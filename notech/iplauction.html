IPL Action

Step into the shoes of a team owner in the thrilling IPL Auction!

Step into the shoes of a team owner in the thrilling IPL Auction! Each team must consist of 3–4 members and is required to strategically select 13–15 players for their squad. Teams will compete to build the best lineup while managing their resources wisely. The excitement runs high, but remember – the host’s decision will be final in all matters. Plan, bid, and assemble a winning team!

Rules & Format

  • Team-based event (2–3 members per team)
  • Three rounds: Cricket Quiz, Auction Simulation, and Match Prediction
  • Quiz includes general knowledge, IPL stats, and live commentary decoding
  • Each team gets a virtual budget to bid for players during the auction
  • Prediction round involves choosing match outcomes based on simulated conditions
  • Fair play and sportsmanship are expected at all times
  • Carry college ID; mobile use during quiz is prohibited
