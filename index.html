ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT :-

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, established in 2002 with an initial intake of 60 then increased to 120 in 2006 is NBA-accredited till 2026 and permanently affiliated to Anna University since 2013. With 24 experienced faculty members, including 12 Ph.D holders and 10 pursuing Ph.D and 2 recognized Ph.D supervisors(Ref. No. 2640062), the department mentors 491 students and several Ph.D scholars—one awarded a Ph.D on 16.09.2022. The department features 6 classrooms, 6 laboratories, an exclusive seminar hall, a Centre of Excellence in AI & IoT, and is supported by 3 technical staff. It has completed AICTE-funded MODROBS and STTP projects worth ₹10 Lakhs and received ₹90 Lakhs for establishing an IDEA Lab. It also secured ₹1 Lakh DST-SERB funding to host a National-Level Conference in November 2022, fostering innovation and research collaboration.

ABOUT THE INSTITUTION :-

Vel Tech High Tech Dr. Rangarajan Dr. Sakunthala Engineering College was established in the year 2002 under R.S.Trust, founded by the Philanthropic couple – Col. Prof. Vel. Dr. R. Rangarajan, Founder President & Chairman and Dr. Sagunthala Rangarajan, Foundress Vice-Chairman respectively. Under the governance of AICTE & Anna University, this College has 10 branches of study with 9 UG Programmes and 2 PG Programmes.
